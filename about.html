About us

  • Global Smog Inc. was established in early 2000. We are a certified smog check station, that also offers brake and lamp inspections and certification, as well as air conditioning services.

    We stay open from 9 am to 6 pm, Monday through Friday, and Saturdays by appointment. We are open longer so you don't have to take off time from work to get your car inspected. Our location has one bay to ensure that your vehicle gets the most accurate, and complete inspect.

    We are an independent family business, meaning that you'll be provided with great service. We do not hire other people to conduct our business. We understand that a single, knowledgeable, ACE trained and certified technician, to get you in and out quickly and as painlessly as possible, will be the best contribution to make your experience pleasant.

    There are NO hidden fees, and our prices are out the door. NO appointment is necessary before you visit us, but if you wish to come outside of working hours, please call and schedule an appointment with our technician. Our station is environmentally safe and green because we hope to make the earth safer for our generation, and for the generations that are to come.

    We take pride in our commitment to speedy, honest services that will cause you to refer your friends and relatives. We value customer satisfaction, and can guarantee the most professional assistance for the best prices.

    We guarantee you will be happy with our services, so come visit us!

    Referrals and repeat customers are the key to our business.
